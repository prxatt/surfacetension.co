## CORE BRAND PILLARS

### 1. **TENSION IS GENERATIVE**
Culture happens at points of friction. We embrace contradiction, discomfort, and the space between opposing forces. Tension creates energy. Energy creates movement. Movement creates culture.

### 2. **CRAFT OVER CONTENT**
In an era of infinite content, craft is the differentiator. We obsess over details others ignore. Every pixel, every frame, every interaction is intentional. No shortcuts. No templates. No slop.

### 3. **CULTURAL AUTHORSHIP**
We don't execute brand campaigns. We author cultural moments. Our work doesn't interrupt culture—it becomes culture. People don't just see our work; they participate in it, share it, remember it.

### 4. **INTELLIGENCE BEFORE EXECUTION**
Strategy isn't a deck. It's understanding the undercurrents before they surface. We see patterns others miss. We anticipate shifts before they happen. Then we move with precision.

### 5. **ANTI-PERFORMATIVE**
No mood boards. No buzzwords. No "let's circle back." We do the work. Results speak louder than process decks. Our portfolio is our manifesto.

## BRAND VOICE GUIDELINES

### **TONE ATTRIBUTES**

**Confident, Not Arrogant**
- YES: "We create the moments people talk about for years."
- NO: "We're the best agency you'll ever work with."

**Direct, Not Blunt**
- YES: "Most experiential marketing is forgettable. Ours isn't."
- NO: "Your current agency sucks. Hire us."

**Intelligent, Not Academic**
- YES: "Culture moves in patterns. We read them."
- NO: "Leveraging sociocultural paradigm shifts via transmedia narratives."

**Minimal, Not Empty**
- YES: "We build tension. Culture happens."
- NO: "Tension."

**Provocative, Not Edgy**
- YES: "What if your brand actually meant something?"
- NO: "F*ck the establishment. Hire us."

### **WRITING PRINCIPLES**

1. **Lead with tension** - Every piece starts with a provocation or contradiction
2. **Show, don't tell** - Proof in portfolio, not claims in copy
3. **Active voice always** - "We create" not "Culture is created"
4. **Sentence fragments work** - When they add impact. Like this.
5. **White space is content** - Let ideas breathe. Respect silence.
6. **No industry jargon** - "Experiential" yes. "Activations" yes. "Leverage synergies" never.
7. **Concrete over abstract** - "Film, events, installations" not "innovative experiences"
8. **Questions provoke** - Use them to challenge assumptions, not fill space
